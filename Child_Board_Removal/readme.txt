[size=12pt][b]Child Board Removal[/b][/size]

[b]Latest Version:[/b] 1.0
[b]Compatible With SMF:[/b] 2.0.x[hr]

Made by: [url=http://www.simplemachines.org/community/index.php?action=profile;u=150637]BurkeKnight[/url] - [url=http://www.burkeknight.com/]BurkeKnight Enterprises[/url]
For Support: The mod's Support topice at SMF, or at [url=http://www.burkeknight.com/]BurkeKnight Enterprises[/url].[hr]

This mod allows you to selectively set your child boards from appearing in their parent's index, they still show as normal inside their parent boards. Simply provide the ID number of the parent whose children you want removed at:
Admin > Configuration > Modification Settings > Child Board Removal
