[b]Redirect Boards to New Tab[/b]

Version: 1.1
Compatibility: 2.0

Made by: [url=http://www.simplemachines.org/community/index.php?action=profile;u=150637]BurkeKnight[/url]

This modification makes it so when people click a board that redirects, it will open in a new tab/window.
